# Groups, Layers, And Sectors

## Purpose

Large workspaces will need organization beyond raw sheet placement. Groups, layers, and sectors are possible ways to manage visibility and meaning in the 2D space.

## Feature Scope

- Group related sheets arbitrarily.
- Show or hide groups.
- Define sectors as meaningful areas in the workspace.
- Navigate or filter by group or sector.

## Open Decisions

- Are sectors user-created objects or inferred from position?
- Should groups affect formulas, visibility only, or both?
- How should grouping interact with navigation history and reference lines?
